Our Mission
Serve The Earth is a web-app dedicated to helping others. We all want to serve others but sometimes we just dont know how or where to go? Serve The Earth allows everyone to serve by donating money to provide items to people that need them. Every week it seems there is another disaster somewhere in the world. Whenever you turn on the news we hear about earthquakes, fires, desises, deaths, and mulitple other disaters and that just doesnt include natural disaters. We look at the supplies that people need and donate towards that according to there needs. Serve The Earth makes that possible. Every three weeks or so we will find a new cause to help out, and each cause will have specific items that you can donate money to that will help someone in need. It doesnt matter how much you donate even a doller will help the world know that we care.

Hope for the holidays

This donation period we are going to donate to the country of Africa. Africa has felt many hardships and in recent months have been affected by ebola. We wanted to give to a country that was facing hard times and feel like we can help the people of Africa in there of need have a hope for the newyear. For this donation period we wanted to donate bikes, first aid kits, and water filters.
Bikes
We want to donate bikes to help those without transportation, not only does it make it easier to get around it it is also safer in many ways. Families will be able to get where they need to in a fast and reliable way.
First Aid Kits
First aid kits help improve the lives of everyone by providing healthy means of getting your body the proper care it needs when needed. No one should have to worry about a small cut getting infected to the point where you need to be hospitalized. This will help many provide a safer and healty life.
Water Bottles
filters provides a way make water drinkable and accesable. We want to provide water bottles that can do that for you. With these water bottles not only will it help health wise it will help families rest easy at night knowing there going to be ok.
