Our Mission

Every week, it seems, there is another disaster happening somewhere in the world. The media reports about earthquakes, fires, diseases, deaths, and natural disasters that are consistently occurring. We all want to serve those in need but sometimes we just don't know how or where to go. "Serve The Earth" allows everyone to serve by donating money that will help provide items to people that need them. Every three weeks "Serve The Earth" will find a new cause to help with. We look at specific supplies that people around the world need and donate to them according to their needs. This website allows you to donate money to these supplies. It doesn't matter how much is donated, even a dollar will help the world know that we care.

Hope for the holidays

This donation period "Serve The Earth" will be donating to the country of Africa. Africa has suffered many hardships and in recent months have been affected by Ebola. With your donation the people of Africa can have hope for the New Year. For this donation period we plan to donate bikes, first aid kits, and water filters.

Bikes
"Serve The Earth" will donate bikes to help those without transportation. With these bikes transportation will be more reliable and as families commute they will be safer.
First Aid Kits
First aid kits will improve the lives of those receiving them by providing a healthy means of proper care. First aid kits would minimize the worry of small cuts getting infected to the point of hospitalization. This will provide a safer and healthier lifestyle.
Water Filters
Water filters provide a way to make water drinkable and accessible. "Serve The Earth" will contribute water filters to families that need assistance and healthy living.
